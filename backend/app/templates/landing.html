About Al-Khutm Project

The Al-Khutm archaeological project represents a significant research endeavor in understanding the ancient history and material culture of the region.

This web platform provides researchers and team members with easy access to excavation data, enabling collaborative research and comprehensive data management. Built on PyArchInit, it integrates seamlessly with QGIS-based archaeological documentation workflows.

  • Complete Data Access: View all excavation records online
  • Media Integration: Associated photos and drawings
  • Advanced Search: Find items by any criteria
